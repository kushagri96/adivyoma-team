About Us

Team Adi Vyoma is the premier student rocketry team representing the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies at the International Rocket Engineering Competition (IREC).

Our core mission is to design, construct, and launch high-performance sounding rockets while advancing experiential aerospace engineering education. By pushing the boundaries of student-led innovation, we aim to deliver a fully functional launch vehicle capable of carrying scientific payloads to exactly 10,000 feet.

Comprising interdisciplinary engineering talent, we handle everything from rigorous aerodynamic simulations and avionics programming to the physical manufacturing of robust aerospace structures.
